More About Tien Tran

I am an Electrical Engineer and Hardware Team Lead at The Signal Center, a company focused on improving health care and health care practices throughout the state of Iowa. I am involved with projects that require Mirocontroller implementation, Sensor networking, and Bluetooth Low Energy communications. With projects that are to be used remotely, I must assure that my work and my team's work will not fail once deployed. Through excellent team diversity and communication, meticulous design and engineering, and a mindset for constant innovation, I strive to deliver unparalleled projects that reflect the passion and dedication given by my team.

As an inquiry-based thinker and a lifelong learner, I aim to pursue further education outside my comfort zone and work with diverse communities to improve myself along with my fellows. There are things I regularly do now that, at one point in my life, I had considered impossible. Having experienced what it truly means to be limitless, I understand now that my future is mine to craft. No matter how grand the task, it can and will be achieved through hard work and unwavering dedication. I now strive to continuously exceed my limits, inspire future lifelong learners and dreamers, and ultimately contribute back to the communities who have shaped me into the man I am today.
